# CSI 33 46 00 - Subdrainage

> **Division:** 33
> **CSI Code:** 33 46 00
> **Title:** Subdrainage
> **Source:** WSU Facilities Services Design & Construction Standards (June 2025)

---

DIVISION 33 - UTILITIES 33 46 00 SUBDRAINAGE
PART 1 - GENERAL
1.01 SCOPE
A. All wall footings, foundations and buried structures shall be provided with subsurface drainage.
PART 2 - PRODUCTS
2.01 MATERIALS
A. Pipe - Foundations drain systems shall be perforated ADS or SDR 35 PVC pipe.
B. Drain Rock - All foundation drains shall be installed in washed drainage aggregate.
C. Filter Fabric - Drain pipe and drain rock shall be protected from silt and fines contamination by enveloping drain rock and pipe in filter fabric.
PART 3 - EXECUTION
3.01 PREPARATION
A. Contractor shall verify foundation waterproofing or dampproofing is complete and that excavation is ready to receive foundation drain system.
3.02 INSTALLATION
A. All foundation drains shall drain to the storm drain system. Do not drain to percolation pits or cisterns, as percolation rates of the native soils are poor.
B. Do not discharge roof drains or other surface runoff into foundation drainage system.
C. Provide cleanouts at all changes in foundation drain direction.
D. Slope all soils away from the structures foundation to provide positive relief from surface runoff.
END OF SECTION
