DIVISION 22 ­ PLUMBING 22 05 00 COMMON WORK RESULTS FOR PLUMBING 22 05 29 HANGERS AND SUPPORTS FOR PLUMBING PIPING AND EQUIPMENT
PART 1 - GENERAL
1.01 PIPE HANGARS AND RESTRAINTS
A. Pipe earthquake restraints shall be designed in accordance with the current USGS Seismic Zone for the each particular WSU campus location.
B. Pipes above suspended ceilings shall still be accessible and at sufficient distance from tile so that the ceiling tiles can be readily removed and replaced for maintenance functions without damaging them.
C. Perforated tape hangers are prohibited.
