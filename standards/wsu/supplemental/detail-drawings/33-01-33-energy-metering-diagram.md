NOTES:
1. MEASUREMENT DEVICES SHALL AT A MINIMUM PROVIDE DAILY DATA AND SHALL BE CONFIGURED TO AUTOMATICALLY COMMUNICATE THE ENERGY DATA TO A DATA ACQUISITION SYSTEM.
2. DATA ACQUISITION SYSTEM SHALL BE CAPABLE OF ELECTRONICALLY STORING THE DATA, FOR A MINIMUM OF 36 MONTHS, FROM THE MEASUREMENT DEVICES AND OTHER SENSING DEVICES CREATING USE REPORTS SHOWING DAILY, MONTHLY AND ANNUAL ENERGY CONSUMPTION.
3. DATA ACQUISITION SYSTEM SHALL BE COMMISSIONED PER THE WASHINGTON STATE ENERGY CODE.
