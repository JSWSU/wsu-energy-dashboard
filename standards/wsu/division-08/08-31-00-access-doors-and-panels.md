DIVISION 08 ­ OPENINGS 08 31 00 ACCESS DOORS AND PANELS
PART 1 - GENERAL
1.01 GENERAL REQUIREMENTS
A. The General Contractor shall be responsible for selection, coordination, and installation of all access doors and panels, whether identified by the Consultant on design drawings or identified by the Contractor as required due to field conditions.
B. Provide access doors or panels wherever equipment access is required for service and maintenance.
1.02 SPECIFIC DESIGN REQUIREMENTS
A. Access doors and panels shall be lockable, and fire-rated to match the assembly in which installed.
B. See section 08 70 00 (Hardware and Keying) for lock and key requirements.
1.03 ACCESS DOORS:
A. Design utility chases with access doors, not access panels.
B. Access doors shall match the materials and finish common to interior doors throughout the building.
1.04 ACCESS PANELS:
A. Minimum access panel size shall be 2' x 2'.
