# CSI 08 14 00 - Wood Doors

> **Title:** Wood Doors
> **Source:** WSU Facilities Services Design & Construction Standards (June 2025)

DIVISION 08 ­ OPENINGS 08 14 00 WOOD DOORS
PART 1 - GENERAL 1.01 SUMMARY A. Exterior 1. In general, wood doors shall not be used for exterior. Proposed exceptions must be approved by the WSU Project Manager. B. Interior 1. Typically, interior doors are to be hardwood-faced, or minimum 0.05 inch laminate plastic-faced, flush, solid core, fire rated as appropriate. 1.02 OTHER REQUIREMENTS A. Provide kickplates where appropriate. B. Use metal door frames throughout.
