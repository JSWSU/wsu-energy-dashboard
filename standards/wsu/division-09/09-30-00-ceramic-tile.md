DIVISION 09 ­ FINISHES 09 30 00 PORCELAIN AND CERAMIC TILING

PART 1 - GENERAL 1.01 SCOPE A. Porcelain tile is the required finish for floors in all restrooms, locker rooms and showers. Epoxy grout is required. B. Porcelain or ceramic tile is the required finish for wainscot in all restrooms, locker rooms and showers. Epoxy grout is required. 1.02 LOCATIONS A. Walls behind lavatories and urinals shall be porcelain or ceramic tile. B. Toilet rooms shall have floor and base of porcelain tile. C. Shower rooms shall have floors, walls and ceilings of ceramic tile. 1.03 ACCESSORIES
A.
1.04 CONSTRUCTION A. Tile floors shall slope ¼" per foot toward the floor drain. B. All unsealed tile shall be sealed with a penetrating sealer if recommended by the tile manufacturer. C. Underlayments or setting products such as waterproofing and/or crack isolation shall be used in all areas that need protection. D. Grout installation: 1. Wet areas (restrooms, shower rooms etc.): Use epoxy grout. 2. Common areas (corridors, entries, lobbies etc.): Stain-resistant grout with polyurethane copolymer is acceptable. i. Acceptable manufacturers: 1) Laticrete Spectralock 2) Flextile Colourmax Plus. 3. Sanded grout is not acceptable. 4. Grout color must be approved by WSU Facilities Services Interior Design.
