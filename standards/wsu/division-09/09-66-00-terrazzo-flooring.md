# CSI 09 66 00 - Terrazzo Flooring

> **Division:** 09
> **CSI Code:** 09 66 00
> **Title:** Terrazzo Flooring
> **Source:** WSU Facilities Services Design & Construction Standards (June 2025)

---

DIVISION 09 ­ FINISHES 09 66 00 TERRAZZO FLOORING
PART 1 - GENERAL
1.01 SCOPE
A. Cementitious terrazzo or epoxy terrazzo are the only acceptable forms of terrazzo at the WSU campus. Terrazzo tile or precast terrazzo are not acceptable.
1.02 CONSTRUCTION
A. All terrazzo base shall be coved with a minimum 1 inch radius. Straight base is not acceptable.
B. Abrasive Stair Strips:
1. Set-in type are required at nosing of all terrazzo stairs. Strips to be 6 inches shorter than stair width. A minimum of two strips of abrasive inserts are required for each stair tread.
PART 2 - PRODUCTS (NOT USED)
PART 3 - EXECUTION (NOT USED)
END OF SECTION
