DIVISION 07 ­ THERMAL AND MOISTURE PROTECTION 07 60 00 FLASHING AND SHEET METAL
PART 1 - GENERAL
1.01 GENERAL REQUIREMENTS
A. See requirements for Parapet Protection and Moisture Protection System Integration with Vertical Surfaces under section 07 00 00 "General Thermal and Moisture Protection."
PART 2 - PRODUCTS
A. New Construction: 50 KSI grade, 24 gauge galvanized steel flashing with factory-applied Polyvinylidene Fluoride (PVDF)
1. Manufacturers: Kynar 500
2. Alternatives require approval by WSU Roofing Shop
B. Equivalent thickness of copper or aluminum is acceptable if required for integration with existing construction.
C. Sealants: One part polyurethane conforming to the current version of FS TTS-00230C, Type II, Class A.
PART 3 - EXECUTION (NOT USED)
END OF SECTION
